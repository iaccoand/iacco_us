I'm a UI/UX guy based out of Ann Arbor Michigan. I believe in user-centered design and writing clean code. My areas of expertise is in designing and building great software interfaces... ones that are easy and delightful to use.

Design

Good design starts with a good design process. A good process focuses on the user, resulting in a design that has been tested and verified, before it any code is written. Here are some techniques I use:

  • UI Sketches - Pencil and paper, markers, whiteboards.
  • Prototypes - Low fidelity and high fidelity prototypes.
  • User Testing - In person task based testing.
  • A|B Testing - Automated testing using software and analytics.

Process

Step 1 Research and Planning - Project requirements, needs of user, business, and dev team.

Step 2 Design - Sketches, wireframes, prototypes.

Step 3 Testing - Verify with users. If the test results require major changes, GO TO STEP 2.

Step 4 Develop - html, css, js, etc.

Step 5 Testing - Verify with users. If major changes, go to step 2. Else if minor changes, go to step 4.
